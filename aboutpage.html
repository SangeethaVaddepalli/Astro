Astro Celebrations is the design culmination of a group of friends who have traveled the world hosting and participating in events of all kinds. They had a collective desire to deliver an opportunity to host memorable events to their hometown area.



ASTRO CELEBRATIONS
is more than just an event hall...

Our 20000 square foot venue is nestled conveniently just south of State Hwy 20 in Forsyth County Georgia. Dramatic 25 foot ceilings adorned with exquisite crystal chandeliers, a beautiful atrium, and gorgeous Georgian marble foyer and so much more, ensure that your guests will feel immediately welcomed into an event of distinction.

The grounds boast two spacious patios and an extraordinary garden complete with a waterfall, all secreted behind a curtain of evergreen trees providing a stunning backdrop and elegant privacy.
